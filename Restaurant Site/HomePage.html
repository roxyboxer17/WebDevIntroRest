The Lakeview Cafe overlooks the Blue River just outside of Dillion Texas. It boasts a beautiful outdoor deck along with an excellent kitchen. The atmosphere is casual and it is a popular destination for tourists and locals. Jasmine and Rusty Jamenson have run the establishment since it was founded in 2001.
